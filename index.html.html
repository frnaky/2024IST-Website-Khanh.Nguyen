Top 5 Japanese Food

Sushi

Sushi is a Japanese dish featuring specially prepared rice and usually some type of fish or seafood, often raw, but sometimes cooked.

Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. It consists of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a broth; common flavors are soy sauce and miso, with typical toppings including sliced pork , nori , menma , and scallions

Sashimi

Sashimi is a Japanese dish of thinly-sliced raw food, usually fish and seafood, but also sometimes other meats.

Tempura

Tempura is a typical Japanese dish usually consisting of seafood, meat and vegetables that have been battered and deep fried.
